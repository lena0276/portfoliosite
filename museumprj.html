Welcome to ‘Reimagining Museums’ virtual exhibition!

This gallery is a virtual representation of my independent research project on immersive technology in museums. This student pin-up exhibition explores six key questions surrounding the integration, incorporation, and implementation of augmented and virtual reality. The purpose of the project was to add to the ongoing debate of the potential for technology to assist museums in creating inclusive, accessible, and equitable visitor experiences.
